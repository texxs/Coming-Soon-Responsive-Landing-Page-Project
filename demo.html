Video and Page load times

Sure, video backgrounds make landing pages load slower. The better the quality and/or the longer the duration of the video, the more it effects load time. Fortunately, just about everyone in America has a high speed internet connection, even on their phones. We all know that phones sometimes don't get great reception and the internet connection is slower then. This is why I decided to substitute an image background for the video backgrounds by default for phones. If you'd like to change this so that video does display on phones, there are simple instructions on the support page.

On a couple of the examples, I've created multiple instances so you can see the difference in quality, loading time and compatibility for yourself. Just explore the links at the bottom of the Rain Splash Video (long version or short version) and the Milky Way Video.
